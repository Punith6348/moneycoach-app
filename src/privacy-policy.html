Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 March 2026

✓ Money Coach does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

1. Overview

Money Coach ("the App") is a personal finance tracking application developed by Turings XYZ Pvt Ltd. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Money Coach does not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Financial data, bank details, or transaction history
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Any other personally identifiable information

3. How Your Data is Stored

All data you enter into Money Coach — including expenses, income, loans, and budgets — is stored exclusively on your own device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

4. No Account Required

Money Coach does not require you to create an account or log in. There is no registration process and we do not have access to any of your financial data.

5. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any data with third parties. Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to share.

6. No Advertising

Money Coach contains no advertisements and does not use any advertising networks or tracking technologies.

7. No Analytics

We do not use any analytics tools, crash reporting services, or tracking SDKs in our application.

8. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset All Data" option in the app's Settings
  • Clearing your browser's local storage
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

9. Children's Privacy

Money Coach is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 18.

10. Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's own security mechanisms. We do not have access to your data and therefore cannot be responsible for any unauthorised access to your device.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
